My life at Kennesaw State University .... Not what you think.

That's right. I'm not your average KSU student. Being a proud growing member of adults choosing to go back to school, I'm proud to have the opportunity to attend KSU. It is said that over 35% of college students are over 25 years old. Out of that 27% of those folks are having to work full time while going to college full time. I belong to that category. Even with all of the years working in corporate and being able to carry all of those life experiences with me, it's still a struggle to do school work. The older you get, the more responsibilities there are and the harder it is to obtain your dreams and aspirations. If I had to give one advice to my fellow KSU classmates, that would be to do it while you're young. You will save time, money, and energy.

If you're in my boat and want more information on KSU as an adult returning to college, check out: Adult Learners @ KSU.

Passion + Golfing = Health Benefits?

The National Golf Foundation reports that in the United States alone, over 42 million people golf annually take part in this sport and it has increased year over year. There are several reasons to do so. It's great for relaxation and health benefits. Golf is placed at a leisurely pace and allows me to take in the beautiful scenery, fresh air, and talk with friends. There is also a lot of walking involved. Walking promotes heart health, improve cholesterol while the golfing posture improves balance. There are many other health benefits such as increase of good mood, bone density and joint health. It is an exercise that brings meaning to your life or at least time away from your family. That itself, everyone should take up golf. Imagine four hrs to yourself! Need I say more...

For more details on how golfing can help improve your health as it did mine, go visit: The Health Benefits of Golf
